Engine: Unity Engine
Github: Treasure of the Sea

Overview

Description

Treasure of the Sea is 3d open-world action-adventure game for single player. The player will craft and gather materials and fight enemies. The main goal of this game is to destroy the boss island. The green color enemy is melee enemy and blue enemy is range enemy. I mainly implemented player controller and enemy AIs. I also developed interaction of objects. I designed the map.
